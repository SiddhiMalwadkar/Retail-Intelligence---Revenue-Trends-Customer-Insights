## Overview

This Power BI dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of retail performance, focusing on revenue trends, customer segmentation, geographic distribution, and payment behavior.

The objective of this project was to transform raw retail transaction data into actionable business insights using structured visualization and analytical storytelling.

---

## Key Business Questions Addressed

- What is the overall revenue performance?
- How are sales distributed across seasons?
- Which cities generate the highest revenue?
- What is the revenue trend over time?
- Which customer segments contribute most to revenue?
- How do customers prefer to pay?
- What is the revenue concentration across locations?
